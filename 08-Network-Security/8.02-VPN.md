# VPN (Virtual Private Network)

## What is VPN?

A VPN creates a secure and encrypted connection between a user and a remote network over the internet.

VPN = Virtual Private Network

---

## Why VPN is Used?

- Secure communication
- Privacy protection
- Remote access
- Secure public WiFi usage

---

## Real-Life Example

Imagine sending a letter through a locked tunnel.

Normal Internet:

Anyone may inspect the letter.

VPN:

Letter travels inside a secure tunnel.

---

## How VPN Works?

Step 1:
User connects to VPN Server

Step 2:
VPN creates encrypted tunnel

Step 3:
Traffic passes through VPN Server

Step 4:
Destination receives request

---

## VPN Components

### VPN Client

Installed on user machine.

### VPN Server

Handles encrypted connections.

### Encryption

Protects transmitted data.

---

## Types of VPN

### Remote Access VPN

Used by employees.

Example:

Work From Home

---

### Site-to-Site VPN

Connects offices.

Example:

Delhi Office ↔ Mumbai Office

---

### SSL VPN

Uses SSL/TLS protocols.

Browser-based VPN.

---

## VPN Protocols

### PPTP

Old protocol.

Less secure.

---

### L2TP/IPSec

Improved security.

---

### OpenVPN

Highly secure.

Widely used.

---

### WireGuard

Modern VPN protocol.

Fast and secure.
